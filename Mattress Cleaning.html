At Variety Steam Cleaning, we specialize in deep cleaning mattresses throughout Johannesburg. Our certified technicians use advanced steam cleaning technology to eliminate dust mites, bacteria, and allergens that accumulate in your mattress over time.

Our Mattress Cleaning Process

We follow a comprehensive 5-step process designed to thoroughly clean and sanitize your mattress:

  • Inspection: We assess your mattress condition and identify problem areas
  • Vacuuming: Powerful suction removes surface dust, hair, and dry particles
  • Pre-treatment: Eco-friendly solutions break down stains and organic matter
  • Steam Cleaning: High-temperature steam penetrates deep to kill bacteria and dust mites
  • Drying: Rapid drying process leaves your mattress fresh and ready for use

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my mattress?

We recommend professional cleaning every 6-12 months for optimal hygiene. Homes with allergy sufferers, pets, or young children may benefit from more frequent cleaning (every 6 months).

Will cleaning remove all stains from my mattress?

While we can significantly improve or remove most stains, success depends on the stain type and age. Organic stains (sweat, urine) typically respond best to treatment. We offer free stain assessment before cleaning.

Is the cleaning process safe for all mattress types?

Yes, our methods are safe for all mattress types including memory foam, latex, and traditional spring mattresses. We adjust moisture levels and cleaning techniques based on your specific mattress.

How long does it take for a mattress to dry?

Drying times vary by mattress type but typically range from 4-8 hours. We recommend using fans or opening windows to accelerate drying. Your mattress will be ready for use the same day.
