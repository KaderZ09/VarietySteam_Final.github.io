Our Tile & Grout Cleaning Process

We follow a meticulous 6-step process to ensure your tiles and grout receive the deepest clean possible:

  • Inspection: We assess your tile condition and identify problem areas
  • Pre-treatment: Specialized solutions loosen embedded dirt and stains
  • Deep scrubbing: High-powered brushes clean grout lines thoroughly
  • Steam cleaning: High-temperature steam sanitizes and removes residues
  • Grout sealing (optional): Protective sealant prevents future staining
  • Final rinse & drying: Leaves surfaces clean and ready for use

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my tiles professionally cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning every month for most households. High traffic areas or homes with children/pets may need cleaning every 1-2 weeks.

Can you remove mold from grout?

Yes, our high-temperature steam cleaning kills mold spores and removes visible mold. We also offer mold-resistant sealants to help prevent future growth.

How long does it take for tiles to dry after cleaning?

Tiles typically dry within 30 min-1 hour/s, while grout may take 4-6 hours depending on humidity and ventilation.

Can you clean all types of tile?

We clean all common tile types including ceramic, porcelain, quarry, and natural stone. Our technicians adjust methods based on your specific tile material.
