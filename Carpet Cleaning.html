Expert Carpet Cleaning Services

At Variety Steam Cleaning, we provide professional carpet cleaning services throughout Johannesburg. Our certified technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly solutions to deep clean your carpets, extending their lifespan and improving indoor air quality.

Our Carpet Cleaning Process

We follow a comprehensive 6-step process to ensure your carpets receive the best possible treatment:

  • Inspection: We assess your carpet type, condition, and specific cleaning needs
  • Pre-vacuuming: Powerful vacuuming removes surface dirt and debris
  • Pre-treatment: Specialized solutions loosen embedded dirt and stains
  • Deep Cleaning: Hot water extraction removes up to 98% of dirt and allergens
  • Spot Treatment: Targeted removal of stubborn stains
  • Protection: Optional stain protector application

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning every 12-18 months for most households. Homes with pets, children, or high traffic may need cleaning every 6-12 months.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after cleaning?

With our advanced extraction methods, carpets typically dry within hours. Using fans or opening windows can help speed up the drying process.

Is your cleaning safe for children and pets?

Yes! We use non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for your entire family once dry (typically 2-4 hours).

Can you remove old, set-in stains?

While we can't guarantee complete removal of all old stains, we have a high success rate with even the toughest, oldest stains using our specialized treatments.
