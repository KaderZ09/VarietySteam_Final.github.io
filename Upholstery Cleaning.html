Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my upholstery?

We recommend professional cleaning every 12-24 months for most households. Homes with pets, children, or light-colored fabrics may need cleaning every 6-12 months.

Will cleaning remove all stains from my furniture?

While we can significantly improve or remove most stains, success depends on the stain type, age, and fabric. We offer free stain assessment before treatment.

Is the cleaning process safe for all fabrics?

We assess each piece individually and use appropriate methods for the specific fabric. Our technicians are trained to handle delicate fabrics like silk and velvet with special care.

How long does it take for upholstery to dry?

Drying times vary by fabric but typically range from 4-8 hours. We recommend opening windows or using fans to accelerate drying.
