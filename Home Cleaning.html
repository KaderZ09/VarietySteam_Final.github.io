Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional home cleaning?

Most clients schedule weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cleanings depending on their needs. Homes with children/pets or high traffic areas may benefit from more frequent service.

What's included in a standard home cleaning?

Our standard cleaning includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming/mopping floors, cleaning bathrooms (toilets, showers, sinks), kitchen cleaning (countertops, appliances), and general tidying.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, we bring all necessary equipment and eco-friendly cleaning products. If you prefer specific products, we're happy to use yours.

How long does a typical home cleaning take?

Duration depends on home size and cleaning type. A standard 3-bedroom home typically takes 3-4 hours for regular cleaning, while deep cleaning may take 5-6 hours.
